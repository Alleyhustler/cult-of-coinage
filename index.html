📜 The Lore of the Cult of Coinage

Long before blockchains, before fiat, before even barter, there were whispers of a hidden brotherhood… a circle that believed value was not given, but summoned.

This brotherhood called itself the Cult of Coinage.
They weren’t farmers, kings, or merchants. They were minters alchemists who believed every coin was a fragment of cosmic truth, every token a relic of fate.

Legends say they built their temples underground, deep vaults carved into stone, where the first symbols of “currency” were etched not on paper, but on sacred ledgers ledgers no eye could see, only believe.

Through the centuries, the Cult moved in silence, guiding empires, sparking markets, collapsing dynasties — always hiding behind their one creed:

“All value is illusion. And illusions belong to us.”
